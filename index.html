What is the Archimedes principle?
Archimedes principle states that the upward buoyant force that is exerted on a body immersed in a fluid whether fully or partially is equal to the weight of the fluid that the body displaces.
How to calculate Archimedes principle?
To calculate Archimedes principle you should use the following formula:
Fb = - ρ * g * v
where
Fb = buoyant force
ρ = fluid density
g = acceleration due to gravity
v = fluid volume
Why use Archimedes principle?
Archimedes principle is used in order to calculate the volume of an object that does not have regualr shape.
